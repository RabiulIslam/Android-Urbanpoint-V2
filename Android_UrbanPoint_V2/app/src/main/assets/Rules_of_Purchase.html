Food & Drink Offers

  1. All Food & Drink offers are valid for dine-in only and are not valid for take-away or delivery orders, unless specifically specified.

  2. A maximum number of 3 offers can be used per table. In case of a discount offer (e.g. 50% Discount), the discount will apply to 3 individual items only, and all other items will be paid at full price.

  3. For “Buy 1 Get 1 Free” offers, up to 3 offers can be used at the same table. For example, if 8 people are dining at the same table, and order 8 main courses, then only the 3 cheapest main courses will be removed from the bill if 3 separate mobile offers are presented. Any additional mobile offers will not be valid.

  4. Offers are not valid for Iftars during Ramadan, theme nights or brunches, unless specifically specified.
